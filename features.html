BMR Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement of body fat based on height and weight that applies to both men and women between the ages of 18 and 65 years.BMI can be used to indicate if you are overweight, obese, underweight or normal. A healthy BMI score is between 20 and 25.


Nutrition Calculator

Description :

In order to calculate the nutrition requirements (calorie consuption) for every day we are using standard BMR calculations.The BMR formula uses the variables of height, weight, age and gender to calculate the Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR). This is more accurate than calculating calorie needs based on body weight alone.

Modification in BMR :

The only factor it omits is lean body mass and thus the ratio of muscle-to-fat a body has. Remember,leaner bodies need more calories than less leaner ones. Therefore, this equation will be very accurate in all but the very muscular (will underestimate calorie needs) and the very fat (will over-estimate calorie needs).But using reliable soruces we have eliminated the percentage of error by adjusting the BMR value according to body fat.
